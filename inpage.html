Company Overview

[榮宗科技] 成立於2012年, 以提供半導體,和光電及面板廠零組件整合性為目標, 致力提供客戶最佳服務!

JCT Tech, established in 2012, supplies parts like quarts, Al2O3, SiC, ceramics, sapphires to Semiconductor, LCD, and LED industries, and provides customers with refurbishing and repairing service.

In addition, JCT Technology focuses on energy conservation and emission reduction, working with our customers as their partner to take up challenges through technology and innovation.
